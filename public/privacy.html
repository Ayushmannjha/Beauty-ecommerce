1. Introduction

Welcome to ShreeAura Private Limited (“ShreeAura”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website (www.shreeaura.com) or any related applications (collectively, the “Platform”).

By using our Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, billing & shipping address, account login details.
  • Payment Information: Processed through secure payment gateways; we do not store card details.
  • Usage Data: Device type, IP address, browser information, pages visited, and interaction data.
  • Optional Data: Feedback, reviews, profile pictures (if provided by you).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate transactional and promotional updates.
  • To improve user experience and platform functionality.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Keep your session active and secure.
  • Remember your preferences and cart items.
  • Analyze traffic and improve performance.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect some site features.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., delivery, payment gateways).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.
  • Analytics and marketing partners (anonymized data only).

6. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When no longer required, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of promotional messages.
  • Withdraw consent for specific data usage.

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@shreeaura.com.

9. Third-Party Services

Our Platform may contain links to third-party services (e.g., payment gateways, social media). We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.
