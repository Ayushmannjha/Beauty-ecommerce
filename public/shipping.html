1. Shipping Cost

All orders are subject to a shipping charge calculated as 18% of the product price. The shipping cost is automatically added to your order total at checkout.

2. Delivery Time

  • Standard delivery typically takes 5–7 business days within India.
  • Delivery times may vary during peak seasons, holidays, or due to unforeseen delays.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can track your order through our Platform or the shipping partner’s website.

4. Shipping Delays

ShreeAura is not responsible for delays caused by shipping partners, customs, or adverse weather conditions. We will provide support and updates wherever possible.

5. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping will be updated on our website when available.
