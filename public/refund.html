1. Eligibility for Return & Refund

Items are eligible for return within 7 days of delivery. The product must:

  • Be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Be in its original packaging with all tags and labels intact.
  • Be accompanied by the original invoice or proof of purchase.

Certain products such as personalized items, hygiene products, and sale items may not be eligible for return or refund.

2. How to Initiate a Return

To return an item:

  • Contact our support team at support@shreeaura.com or call us at [customer care number].
  • Provide your order ID, product details, and reason for return.
  • Once approved, we’ll arrange for pickup or provide return instructions.

3. Refund Process

  • After receiving and inspecting the returned item, we will notify you about the refund status.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 5–7 business days.
  • COD orders will be refunded via bank transfer or store credit.

4. Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges are only offered for defective or damaged items.
  • Contact us within 48 hours of receiving the order to request an exchange.

5. Late or Missing Refunds

  • Check your bank account again.
  • Contact your bank or payment provider.
  • If still unresolved, email us at support@shreeaura.com.

6. Cancellations

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order or before shipment.
  • Once shipped, the order cannot be cancelled.
